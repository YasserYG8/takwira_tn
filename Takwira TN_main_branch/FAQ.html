Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ section! Here, you'll find answers to the most common questions about using our platform.

How do I create an account on Takwira TN ?

Simply click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in your details such as name, email, and password, and you’re ready to start exploring the platform

Do I need to sign up to use Takwira TN ?

Yes, signing up allows you to access all our features, including booking fields, finding teammates, and managing your games.

How can I find stadiums near me ?

Use the "Browse" feature to search for stadiums based on your location, preferred sport, and availability.

Can I view the availability of stadiums before booking ?

Absolutely! Our platform provides real-time availability, so you can pick a time slot that works best for you.

What is the matchmaking feature ?

Matchmaking helps you find players to complete your team based on filters like location, and availability.

How do I book a field ?

After finding your preferred stadium, select an available time slot and proceed to confirm your reservation.

What payment methods are supported ?

We offer secure payment options, including credit/debit cards, online wallets and especially D17, ensuring a smooth transaction process.

Is Takwira TN free to use ?

While basic features are free, we also offer premium plans for additional benefits like ad free experience
